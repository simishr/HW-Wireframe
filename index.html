About Me

Hi! my name is Smriti Shrestha. I am originally from Nepal, born and raised in the capital - Kathmandu. Currently, I live in Jersey City. I did my Bachelors and Masters in Management with a major in finance and marketing respectively.

Back home, I was also helping out in the family business of Interior services and Furniture manufacturing as an Assistant Designer and Project Manager. After coming to the United States it took me a while to figure out what I wanted to do next in my life. My curiosity led me to join Rutgers Coding Bootcamp which I consider is the best step in my life so far!
